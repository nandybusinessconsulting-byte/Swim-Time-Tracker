SwimTrack

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

SwimTrack does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter stays on your device.

What data the app uses

SwimTrack stores the following information locally on your device only, using your phone's built-in secure storage (AsyncStorage):

  • Swimmer names, birth years, and gender
  • Logged swim times and meet names
  • App preferences (selected age group, course type, dark/light mode)

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

What data we do NOT collect

  • No account registration or login
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No crash reporting transmitted off-device

Camera & Photos (optional)

If you choose to add a profile photo to a swimmer, the app may request access to your camera or photo library. This permission is entirely optional. No photos are transmitted or stored outside your device.

SwimCloud meet data

When you open the "New Meet" sheet, the app fetches a list of upcoming NJ meets from our API server, which in turn retrieves publicly available meet listings from SwimCloud. No personal information is sent in this request — it is a simple public data lookup.

Third-party services

SwimTrack does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Data deletion

All app data is stored on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. No action on our end is required.

Children's privacy

SwimTrack is designed for use by parents tracking their children's swim times. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults. All data remains on-device at all times.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. We will not make changes that affect data handling without updating this page.
